My favorites... no filter needed.

Life is an adventure, and I am an adventurer.   Every day – every moment – has the potential to be something great.   When given the opportunity to discover something new, explore an unknown location, or face a new challenge, my answer is always "yes!"   I am no photographer, yet I take hundreds of pictures each year.   You see, the human brain can only hold so many memories, but I want to remember them all!   This collection of photos features some of my favorite people, places, and things to do.   They are all dear to me – enjoy!

Family

I can't talk about my favorites without mentioning my city.   I've lived in several beautiful and interesting places, but St. Louis has always been home to me.   It's where I belong – my comfort zone. Here are a few of the local spots I love most.

The Gateway Arch

St. Louis' iconic Gateway Arch stands 630 feet tall, and the views from the top are amazing!

View from the top

Missouri Botanical Gardens

Founded in 1859, Missouri Botanical Gardens is conisidered by many to be one of top three botanical gardens in the world.

Blown glass "onions" adorn the lily pond

The St. Louis Zoo

Located in beautiful Forest Park, the St. Louis Zoo showcases more than 16,000 animals and is ranked as one of America's best.
